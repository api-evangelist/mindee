Optical Mark Recognition (OMR) has evolved from rigid, hardware-dependent machines into flexible cloud-based solutions. The technology detects marked data on forms and continues to power workflows like election counting and standardized testing, while modern APIs eliminate the need for expensive specialized scanners.
