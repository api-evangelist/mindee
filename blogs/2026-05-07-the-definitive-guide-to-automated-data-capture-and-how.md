Manual data entry carries error rates between 1-5%, which becomes critical at enterprise scale. Automated data capture systems using AI and intelligent document processing replace manual keystrokes with intelligent recognition, extracting structured data from unstructured documents like PDFs and photos while reducing errors and operational costs.
