On-premise OCR executes locally on your hardware while cloud OCR processes data on remote servers. Local deployments guarantee data sovereignty and control but require heavy DevOps overhead, whereas cloud solutions offer instant integration and continuous machine learning improvements but require trusting external vendors with sensitive payloads.
